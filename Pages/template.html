Our Mission. To Help People in Distress

Sometimes in life, some situations become challenging and burdensome.. and during those times we just wish if someone would listen without judging us. Such hard times can now be easily walked through with our help.AnonyFriend is a platform extending free support to people experiencing emotional distress by connecting with them. I am a trained listener, trained in active listening and I will interact with you via an anonymous and confidential chat. Anonymously sharing our problems with trained ears sometimes make the walk through the rough road easier. You can also share it with your friends or someone whom you know is going through a difficult time. After all helping people around us is always a good deed.

What We Do. We help

Nomatter what, you must never feel alone because we are always here for you. Sometimes personally visiting therapists isnt the most convienient option for us and that's where we step in. Bridging all boundaries and crossing all barriers, through this instant connect service, you can easily write to me and connect. I will hear you out, try to give the best help and will always be your anonymous friend.Your one click-away-friend cum therapist.
